The LINQ Hotel + Experience

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $110

  • Modern rooms with clean, sleek designs.
  • Prime location near top attractions on the Strip.
  • Vibrant entertainment options, including a rooftop pool.

The LINQ Hotel + Experience is a standout among 3-star hotels in Las Vegas, offering affordability without compromising on quality. Located in the heart of the Strip, this property features modern, stylish rooms equipped with essential amenities like flat-screen TVs and Wi-Fi. The LINQ’s central location makes it perfect for travelers seeking an exciting and budget-friendly stay in Las Vegas.
