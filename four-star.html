Elevate Your Stay with 4-Star Hotels in Las Vegas

Upgrade your Las Vegas experience with exceptional 4-star hotels that combine style, comfort, and prime locations. Top 4 Star Hotels Las Vegas

Park MGM Las Vegas

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $200

  • Non-smoking property with a sleek, modern design.
  • Multiple dining options, including Eataly food hall.
  • Relaxing pools and live entertainment venues.

Park MGM Las Vegas offers a refined, contemporary atmosphere ideal for those seeking an upgraded experience in the heart of the Strip. This non-smoking property features stylish accommodations with vibrant décor and modern amenities. Guests can enjoy world-class dining, including the renowned Eataly, and access live performances at the Dolby Live Theater. Its central location and peaceful ambiance make it a favorite among travelers looking for a premium stay in Las Vegas.

The Mirage Hotel & Casino

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $210

  • Famous volcano show and tropical-inspired theme.
  • Spacious rooms with modern amenities.
  • On-site entertainment and multiple dining options.

The Mirage Hotel & Casino combines tropical charm with the excitement of Las Vegas. Known for its iconic volcano show, this resort offers spacious accommodations with updated furnishings and premium amenities. Guests can enjoy a range of entertainment options, from comedy shows to the Beatles LOVE by Cirque du Soleil, as well as a variety of restaurants. Its prime location on the Strip ensures easy access to other top attractions.

Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $195

  • Trendy, Hollywood-inspired décor throughout.
  • Large casino floor and a vibrant nightlife scene.
  • Direct access to the Miracle Mile Shops.

Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino delivers a fun, energetic atmosphere with its Hollywood-themed rooms and lively entertainment options. The property is home to some of the best nightlife venues on the Strip and provides direct access to the Miracle Mile Shops, featuring over 170 stores and restaurants. With stylish accommodations and an unbeatable location, this resort is perfect for those looking to mix luxury and excitement.

Paris Las Vega

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $220

  • European-inspired design with a replica Eiffel Tower
  • Elegant accommodations and fine dining options.
  • Central location with breathtaking views of the Strip.

Paris Las Vegas brings a touch of French elegance to the bustling Las Vegas Strip. Featuring a replica Eiffel Tower and charming Parisian décor, the resort offers sophisticated rooms, upscale dining, and excellent entertainment options. Guests can dine at the Eiffel Tower Restaurant or enjoy casual fare while taking in stunning views of the Strip. Its central location makes it an excellent choice for travelers seeking both style and convenience.

The Venetian Resort

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $280

  • All-suite accommodations with luxurious amenities.
  • Gondola rides and upscale shopping at Grand Canal Shoppes
  • Multiple award-winning restaurants.

The Venetian Resort is a standout 4-star property that feels like a world-class escape. Offering all-suite accommodations, the resort is known for its opulent design and attention to detail. Guests can enjoy a romantic gondola ride or explore the Grand Canal Shoppes featuring luxury brands. With its exquisite dining options and exceptional service, The Venetian is perfect for travelers seeking a memorable and indulgent stay.

Mandalay Bay Resort and Casino

Location: South Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $250

  • Famous Mandalay Bay Beach with wave pool and lazy river.
  • Variety of dining options and world-class entertainment.
  • Spacious, modern rooms with excellent views.

Mandalay Bay Resort offers a mix of relaxation and entertainment with its stunning beach complex and luxurious accommodations. Guests can unwind at the wave pool or enjoy top-tier performances at the Michelob Ultra Arena. With an array of dining options and stylish rooms, Mandalay Bay provides a versatile and comfortable experience for travelers looking for more than just gaming.

New York-New York Hotel & Casino

Location: South Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $190

  • Iconic replica of the New York skyline.
  • Roller coaster and unique dining experiences
  • Fun, themed atmosphere perfect for families and couples.

New York-New York Hotel & Casino brings the Big Apple to Las Vegas with its themed design and exciting attractions. From the thrilling Big Apple Roller Coaster to classic New York-style pizza, the property provides a fun and immersive experience. Its affordable luxury and lively atmosphere make it a great choice for travelers wanting an upbeat Vegas stay.

The Cromwell

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $240

  • Boutique-style hotel with a modern, chic design.
  • Rooftop pool and exclusive Drai's Beachclub & Nightclub.
  • Central location near top attractions.

The Cromwell is a boutique hotel offering an intimate and upscale experience in the heart of Las Vegas. Its stylish accommodations feature custom furnishings and thoughtful touches. Guests can enjoy stunning rooftop views at Drai's Beachclub or dine at the acclaimed Giada restaurant. With its central location, this property is perfect for travelers seeking a unique and sophisticated stay.

Treasure Island Hotel & Casino

Location: Center Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $200

  • Comfortable rooms with modern amenities.
  • Home to Mystère by Cirque du Soleil.
  • Excellent location with easy access to shopping and dining.

Treasure Island Hotel blends comfort and entertainment, making it a fantastic 4-star option for visitors. The property’s comfortable rooms and convenient location appeal to those seeking value without sacrificing quality. With the Mystère show and various dining options, Treasure Island ensures a dynamic and enjoyable experience.

Resorts World Las Vegas

Location: North Strip

Price Per Night: Starting at CAD $275

  • Brand-new property with modern, upscale design
  • Multiple hotels under one roof (Hilton, Conrad, and Crockfords)
  • Extensive dining options and cutting-edge entertainment.

Resorts World Las Vegas is one of the newest additions to the Strip, offering a cutting-edge experience with a range of accommodations to suit different tastes. Guests can enjoy luxurious rooms, world-class dining, and state-of-the-art entertainment. With its modern design and high-end amenities, Resorts World is a top pick for travelers seeking the latest and greatest in Las Vegas.
